Pricing FAQ

Quick Answers.

Is the Free plan really forever?
Yep. You can stay on Free as long as you want. Most creators upgrade once they have 2+ active brand deals and want a real pipeline + invoices in one place.
What happens after the 14-day Pro trial?
If you don't add a card, you drop back to Free automatically — no charges, no canceled deals. Your data stays put. Upgrade whenever.
Do you take a cut of my brand deals?
Never. We're not an agency. Brands message you directly, you negotiate the price, you keep 100% of every deal. Period.
Can I switch between monthly and annual?
Anytime. Switching to annual prorates the discount. Switching back to monthly takes effect at the next billing cycle.
What's included in a brand campaign package?
Creator sourcing, briefing, video production, multiple hooks & variations, and delivery in spec for your platform. Usage rights and whitelisting are included on Growth and up.
Do you do international brands or campaigns?
Yes. Our roster is US-heavy but we have creators in the UK, Canada, and Australia. International brand campaigns are billed in USD.
Is there a refund policy?
For creator subscriptions: cancel anytime, prorated refund within the first 30 days. For brand campaigns: deposit is refundable until creators are matched.
