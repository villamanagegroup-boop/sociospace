Quick Answers

Before You Sign Up.

Do you take a cut on creator deals?
Not on marketplace deals. When a brand books a creator direct through the roster, the creator keeps 100% of their rate. We make money on creator and brand subscriptions. The only exception: brands who hire our agency to run a managed campaign — that's a separate quote, agreed up front.
Is there a minimum follower count to join?
No hard minimum. What matters more is content quality, niche fit, and reliability. An engaged 5k can outperform a flat 100k every time.
How does it work for brands?
Browse the roster free. When you're ready to message a creator or post a job, upgrade to Pro ($99/mo) or Pro+ ($199/mo). If you'd rather hand off the whole campaign, our agency runs it end-to-end — get in touch for a custom quote.
Marketplace or agency — which one am I using?
Both options are open. Most brands self-serve through the marketplace: search, unlock, message, hire direct. When a campaign needs strategy, scripting, production, or paid amplification, that's our agency side — contact us and we'll scope it.
