Pricing FAQ

Quick Answers.

Is the Free plan really forever?
Yep. You can stay on Free as long as you want. Most creators upgrade once they have 2+ active brand deals and want a real pipeline + invoices in one place.
What happens after the 14-day Pro trial?
If you don't add a card, you drop back to Free automatically — no charges, no canceled deals. Your data stays put. Upgrade whenever.
Do you take a cut of my brand deals?
Not on marketplace deals. When a brand finds you through the roster and books you direct, you keep 100% — they pay their subscription, you keep your rate. The only time we earn on a deal is when a brand hires our agency to run a managed campaign and you're cast through us.
Can I switch between monthly and annual?
Anytime. Switching to annual prorates the discount. Switching back to monthly takes effect at the next billing cycle.
Brands — what does the Free plan get me?
Full marketplace access — browse the entire creator roster, see niches, rates, portfolio samples, and save creators to private lists. Handles and contact info stay hidden until you upgrade. No card required.
How do contact unlocks work?
On Pro, each unlock reveals a creator's handle and opens a direct message thread. You get 10 per month. On Pro+, unlimited. Used unlocks reset on the 1st.
Do you take a cut of brand deals?
Not on marketplace deals — when you message a creator direct and book them through the roster, no commission. We make money on brand subscriptions. If you hand a campaign to our agency to run end-to-end, that's a separate quote.
Is there a refund policy?
Cancel anytime — prorated refund within the first 30 days. After that, your subscription stays active through the period you've paid for and won't renew.
