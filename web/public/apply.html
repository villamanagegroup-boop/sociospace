Common Questions

Before You Apply.

Is it really free?
Yes. The roster listing, media kit, deal pipeline, and invoice generator are free forever. Pro ($29/mo) and Pro+ ($49/mo) unlock unlimited deals tracked, advanced analytics, and priority placement — but you can run your business on Free for as long as you want.
Do you take a cut on brand deals?
Not on marketplace deals. When a brand books you direct through the roster, you keep 100% of your rate. We make money on creator and brand subscriptions. The only exception: if a brand hires our agency to run a managed campaign and you're cast through us, the rate is set together upfront.
How long does the review take?
48 hours, max. We review every application by hand. If we have questions, we'll email you. If you're a fit, you'll get a setup link to build your profile and start showing up in brand searches.
Do I need a minimum follower count?
No hard minimum. What matters more is your content quality, niche fit, and reliability. An engaged 5k can outperform a flat 100k every time.
What if I don't show up on camera?
UGC (user-generated content) is one of our biggest categories — many creators on the roster never show their face. Voice-overs, hands-on-product, faceless tutorials, AI avatar work — all welcome.
Can I leave the roster anytime?
Yes. Pause your profile or remove it entirely from your dashboard — no questions, no fees, no exit interview. Your past deal history stays exportable for taxes.
